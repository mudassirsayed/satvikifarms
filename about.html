ABOUT US

The word "Satviki" means "purity" in Sanskrit. The name showcases/represents both our values and our company's distinguishing characteristics. Satviki farms is a locally operated hydroponic farm that produces fresh, clean and pesticide-free greens. We are located at Worli, Mumbai. With the smart farming technique, we grow greens in a better, fresher and a cleaner way.
